You should have gone through some school, coaching or/and college? Here are we, working on a web App where we are trying to bring everything related to these places online. Simply, we are trying to keep track of the academic journey of everyone.   more

There are some things we are planning to achieve,
1.Providing Freshers/Juniors an easy way to know who are their seniors and interact with them.
2. One can easily get to know any student from their branch or other in their college or any other college, if that has an account on schools.
3. A news feed will update you with various events going in various colleges around the nation and abroad.
4. After you get out of college, you are doing some job and you had like to go through your college days, you can do so on schools app. You always have access to every guy you studied with in your school, college or coaching (They just need to have an account on schools app).
5. This app will keep track of one's academic jouney.
6. If you have a friend studying in other college than you, you can find out who he/her is studying with and what different activities are happening in his/her college.
And a lot more exciting things, you will surely love to go through.
